UArizona CyberCats

We are dedicated to providing students at the University of Arizona with hands-on, cutting-edge cybersecurity experiences while also providing networking opportunities with industry professionals.

What We Do

Weekly Workshops

We hold weekly, virtual workshop sessions on the 1st, 2nd, and 4th Thursdays of the month.

Conferences

Jointly attend conferences as a club like Defcon and CactusCon.

Professional Networking

Monthly Joint Meetings with the local (ISC)^2 chapter on the 3rd Thursday of the month.

Trainings and Support

We provide trainings on tools and practices and a robust support group of students avaliable to answer questions via Discord.
